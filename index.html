Retro Games

snake

Play Snake, the iconic arcade game, and guide your growing snake to eat food while avoiding collisions. Control the reptile with either arrow keys or WASD for a thrilling challenge.

pong

Enjoy a two-player Pong showdown with Player One on 'W' and 'S,' and Player Two on arrow keys. Brace for intensified fun as the ball accelerates by 0.6 after each collision. First player to 10 Wins.

TicTacToe

Engage in Tic Tac Toe, a strategic duel where players mark spaces in a 3x3 grid. Victory awaits those who cleverly align three symbols horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Enjoy the challenge.
